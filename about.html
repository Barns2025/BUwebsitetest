About Barns Unlimited

Barns Unlimited was founded with a passion for quality barn building and restoration. We are a family-owned company based in Lima, Ohio, and we pride ourselves on combining old-fashioned craftsmanship with modern building techniques. Our team has decades of experience in designing, constructing, and refurbishing barns of all kinds.

From humble beginnings, we have grown into a full-service barn construction and renovation firm. We specialize in pole barns, vintage barn restorations, and even steel-frame hybrid structures. No project is too large or too small – whether you need a simple storage barn or a sprawling equestrian facility, our experts will ensure the result exceeds your expectations.

Our Mission & Values

Commitment to Sustainability: We incorporate sustainable practices and materials in our projects whenever possible, ensuring environmental responsibility without compromising quality.

Historic Preservation: We respect historic barns and preserve their heritage while reinforcing them for continued use.

Quality Craftsmanship: Each barn is treated as our own, using quality materials and attention to detail.

Customer Satisfaction: Honest communication and going the extra mile from design to walkthrough.

Why Choose Barns Unlimited?

  • Experience: Over 20 years in the industry.
  • Full-Service: Design, site prep, construction, and finishing.
  • Licensed & Insured: Peace of mind throughout the build.
  • Local & Family-Owned: We treat you like a neighbor.

When you choose Barns Unlimited, you’re partnering with a dedicated team to make your dream barn a reality.
